Data Deletion

You can delete your data and revoke access at any time.

Option A — In-App

  1. Open NOISEMAKER → Settings > Account → Delete Account.
  2. Confirm deletion. We queue deletion immediately and purge active records within 24–72 hours; backups within 30–90 days.

Option B — Revoke TikTok Access

  1. Go to TikTok → Settings > Security > Apps & Websites.
  2. Find “NOISEMAKER by DooWopp” and revoke access.

Option C — Email Request

Email support@doowopp.com with the subject “Data Deletion Request.” Include your account email and TikTok handle.

Note: We may retain minimal records required by law (e.g., invoices, fraud prevention) and will confirm when deletion is complete.
